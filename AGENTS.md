# Project Brief: Salud Mental Venezuela - Earthquake Relief (Venezuela)

## 1. Project Overview
**Salud Mental Venezuela** is a crisis response landing page designed to connect victims of the recent Venezuelan earthquake with professional mental health support. The platform serves as a bridge between individuals in distress and a network of volunteer psychologists and counselors.

## 2. Objective
To provide a simple, accessible, and empathetic entry point for affected individuals to request psychological assistance, ensuring their data is securely routed to the coordination team.

## 3. Target Audience
- **Primary:** Individuals in Venezuela affected by the recent seismic activity.
- **Secondary:** Family members seeking support for loved ones.
- **Context:** Users are likely experiencing high stress, anxiety, or trauma, and may be accessing the site via mobile devices with potentially unstable internet connections.

## 4. Visual Strategy (Serene Support)
- **Mood:** Empathetic, trustworthy, calm, and professional.
- **Color Palette:** 
  - Primary: Deep Teal (#00796b) for stability and professional trust.
  - Backgrounds: Soft grays and off-whites to reduce visual fatigue.
- **Typography:** Manrope (Sans-serif) for high legibility on mobile screens.
- **Design Principles:** Minimalist layout, generous whitespace, and clear "Safe" indicators.

## 5. Functional Requirements
### Section 1: Hero & Introduction
- **Headline:** Direct and supportive (e.g., "Encuentra Apoyo Profesional").
- **Copy:** Brief acknowledgment of the situation and a clear value proposition of the professional support offered.
- **Language:** Spanish (Local context).

### Section 2: Intake Form
- **Fields:**
  - Full Name (Nombre completo)
  - Age (Edad)
  - Location (Ubicación: Ciudad/Estado)
  - Preferred Contact Method (WhatsApp, Phone, Email)
  - Phone Number
  - Email Address
- **Submission:** Single-action "Enviar solicitud" button.
- **Data Routing:** All submissions are routed to `carlosesierra@gmail.com`.
- **Privacy:** Footer note confirming confidential handling of information.

## 6. Technical Specifications
- **Device Target:** Mobile-first responsive design.
- **Performance:** Lightweight assets for fast loading in areas with limited connectivity.
- **Accessibility:** High contrast text and large touch targets for the form fields.

## 7. Success Metrics
- Successful form completions.
- Mobile responsiveness across varied screen sizes.
- Positive feedback on the clarity and empathy of the messaging.
